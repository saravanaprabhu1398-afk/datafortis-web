Who We Are

DataFortis is a leading data infrastructure consulting company specializing in building scalable, real-time data platforms for enterprise clients. Founded by industry veterans with decades of experience in data engineering, we help organizations transform their data operations from traditional batch processing to modern streaming architectures.

Our mission is to empower businesses with robust data infrastructure that enables real-time decision-making, reduces costs, and unlocks the full potential of their data assets.
